Reading

Reading is something I enjoyed as a kid, and at the time I enjoyed it simply because I loved storytelling.

As I got older, I realised I liked the fact that I get to experience life through the lens of another. It made me aware of realities outside of mine.

In recent times, it has become harder to read due to a lot of distractions and attention span. However, I have grown to like audio books and I love that I can listen to a book while performing a chore.

Benefits of reading

To expand your knowledge.

To expand your vocabulary.

I believe reading certain books, especially fiction, increases ones ability to empathize. I believe reading amongst others, has helped understand there are realities and beliefs outside of mine, and I've learnt to respect and empathise where needed.
